Retainuser CRM is a cloud-based CRM software developed by Test User Pvt Ltd that helps businesses manage leads, automate follow-ups, and track sales performance.

Retainuser CRM helps businesses capture leads, organize customer data, automate communication, and improve sales conversions through a centralized system.

Retainuser CRM allows capturing leads from website forms, Facebook Ads, and third-party platforms like 99acres and housing.com.

It provides automated follow-up reminders so sales teams never miss customer interactions.

It enables scheduling meetings and follow-ups with customers on specific dates.

It offers a sales pipeline dashboard to track deals and monitor progress from lead to closure.

It supports WhatsApp automation to communicate with customers instantly.

It provides centralized customer data storage accessible to the entire team.

It includes role-based access so managers and employees have controlled permissions.

It offers cloud-based access so users can log in from anywhere anytime.

Retainuser CRM helps businesses increase sales conversions, improve team productivity, and reduce manual work through automation.

It ensures that no lead is missed and every customer is followed up on time.

It improves decision-making by providing clear insights into sales performance.

Retainuser CRM is widely used in industries such as real estate, insurance, education, solar businesses, service providers, SMEs, and sales teams.

It is especially useful for businesses that handle large volumes of leads and need structured follow-up processes.
